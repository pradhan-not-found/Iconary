# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Iconary is a welcoming space for contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels — including first-time open source contributors. We want everyone to feel comfortable submitting their first PR, even if it's not perfect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in reviews and discussions.
- Assume good intent — most mistakes are honest, especially from newcomers.
- Give specific, actionable feedback on PRs rather than just rejecting them.
- Credit contributors for their work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Dismissive or condescending responses to beginner contributions.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a confidential report by contacting the maintainers directly (add contact method here, e.g., a dedicated email).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close PRs/issues, or temporarily/permanently ban contributors who violate this code of conduct.

---

This Code of Conduct is intentionally short and will evolve as the community grows.
